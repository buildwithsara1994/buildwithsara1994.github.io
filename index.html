Sara Haghighi 🐱

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Maine School of Computing and Information Science, specializing in privacy engineering. My research focuses on aligning user expectations, developer practices, and privacy regulations to build systems that are secure, compliant, and usable.

I work under the supervision of Dr. Sepideh Ghanavati, whose research focuses on privacy engineering, regulatory compliance, and requirements engineering.

As part of my doctoral work, I have contributed to multiple research projects exploring the intersection of software engineering, privacy regulations, and artificial intelligence. Since May 2023, I have worked as a Research Assistant at the University of Maine, conducting studies on developer privacy practices, privacy requirements, and the use of large language models for privacy-aware software engineering.

Earlier in my doctoral program, I served as a Teaching Assistant at the University of Maine from Fall 2022 to May 2023, supporting courses including Programming Languages and Foundations of Computer Science.
